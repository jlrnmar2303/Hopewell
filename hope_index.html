Youth group needs help!

Helping the youth group go to camp is easy! The Hopewell youth group will be hosting a yard sale-craft bazar-bake sale to raise money for camp. If you have anything that you would like to donate for the sale, please bring it to church or we can arrange for pick up if needed. In addition to yardsale items, we are collecting crafts and bake sale items. The sale will be held in the church and advertised through out the community and on social media. If you have any suggestions to help improve our event or any questions please feel free to contact the youth committee.
